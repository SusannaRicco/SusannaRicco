# Susanna Ricco

I am a Senior Staff Research Engineer at Google DeepMind, currently serving as the Safety Lead for Gemini Image Generation. My work focuses on the intersection of frontier generative AI and responsible development, ensuring that large-scale multimodal models are both helpful and safe for global users.

With over a decade of experience at Google and a Ph.D. in Computer Science, I have dedicated my career to advancing Computer Vision and Machine Learning from initial research to planet-scale production.

### Core Focus and Research

* **Generative AI Safety:** Leading alignment and safety frameworks for Gemini’s image generation capabilities, focusing on robust evaluation and mitigation of downstream risks.
* **Computer Vision:** Expertise in image understanding, visual tracking, and the synthesis of high-fidelity generative content.
* **Scalable AI Engineering:** Transitioning complex research models into stable, production-ready systems that serve millions.
